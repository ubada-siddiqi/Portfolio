My process.

Sharing my process builds trust, showcasing my unique approach and problem-solving skills. It's not just about what I do; it's about how I do it, making my portfolio memorable and credible.

  • Discovery
  • Design
  • Development
  • Deployment

Discovery:

- Conduct thorough research to understand client needs, project objectives, and target audience.
- Gather relevant data, conduct competitor analysis, and define project scope.
- Establish clear goals and expectations, ensuring a solid foundation for the design phase.

Design:

- Develop wireframes outlining the website's structure and layout.
- Create detailed mockups to visualize the user interface and incorporate client feedback.
- Design elements such as color schemes, typography, and imagery to enhance visual appeal.
- Ensure a user-friendly experience with a focus on navigation and accessibility.

Development

- Translate design elements into a functional website using coding languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Implement responsive design to ensure optimal performance across various devices.
- Conduct thorough testing to identify and resolve any bugs or issues.
- Collaborate with clients to fine-tune details and make necessary adjustments.

Deployment

- Launch the website, ensuring a seamless transition from development to the live environment.
- Perform final testing to confirm functionality, compatibility, and responsiveness.
- Provide necessary training for clients or end-users on maintaining and updating the website.
- Monitor post-launch performance and address any additional needs or optimizations.
